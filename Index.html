About Us

Booktopia.com is the largest and most complete online bookstore in Indonesia that provides a variety of quality books. Since 2018, Booktopia has built an online store. This store is part of the Booktopia Store. From 2018 to the present, Booktopia.com is managed by PT.Ethereal Books. Now Booktopia.com has been integrated with more than 90 Booktopia store branches throughout Indonesia. Customers can shop and make purchases from the nearest Booktopia in your city.
